# Matte Cold Outreach Scripts

## Email Sequence

### Email 1: Initial Outreach
**Subject lines (A/B test):**
- "Quick question about [COMPANY NAME]"
- "Saw your work in [CITY] - question"
- "How are you tracking jobs right now?"

**Body:**
```
Hey [FIRST NAME],

Found [COMPANY NAME] while looking at painters in [CITY] - your work looks solid.

Quick question: how are you currently tracking your estimates and jobs? Whiteboard? Texts? Spreadsheet?

I built a dead-simple app called Matte specifically for painting crews under 10 people. No bloated features, no learning curve - just estimates, jobs, and getting paid.

Takes 5 minutes to see if it's useful for you. Want me to send a quick demo video?

- Flip
```

---

### Email 2: Follow-up (3 days later)
**Subject:** Re: Quick question about [COMPANY NAME]

**Body:**
```

Following up - I know you're busy on jobs.

Here's the 30-second pitch: Matte lets you create estimates on your phone, convert them to jobs with one tap, and track everything without switching between 5 apps.

One painter told me he was losing $2-3K/month in jobs that "fell through the cracks" before using it.

Worth a 10-minute call this week?

- Flip
```

## Phone Script (if you call)

**Opening:**
"Hey [NAME], this is Flip - I sent you an email about a job tracking app for painters. Got 60 seconds?"

**If yes:**
"Cool. Quick question - when a customer calls for an estimate, what do you do with that info right now? Write it down? Text yourself?"

*[Let them talk - this reveals their pain]*

**Transition:**
"Got it. So I built this app called Matte because I kept hearing painters say the same thing - estimates get lost, jobs fall through the cracks, invoicing is a nightmare. 

It's dead simple - create estimate on your phone, convert to job, track it, get paid. No BS features you'll never use.

Want me to show you in like 5 minutes?"

---

## Key Talking Points

**Pain points to hit:**
- Lost estimates = lost money
- Texting/notes = chaos
- Big software = overkill and expensive
- End of day = can't remember what you quoted

**Differentiators:**
- Built FOR painters, not adapted from general contractor software
- Mobile-first (because you're on job sites, not at a desk)
- Simple pricing, no per-user BS
- Actually simple (5 min to learn, not 5 weeks)
